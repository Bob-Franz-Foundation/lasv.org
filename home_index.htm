Welcome to LASV.org, the portal of the Los Altos Academy of Engineering, LAVROP Manufacturing Technology, the Los Altos Solar Vehicle Program, Los Altos Solar Tech Club and ISAAQ.

This is a class/club formed by the La Puente Valley ROP and Los Altos High School. This club is run mainly by the students who have dedicated their time to this cause. The school is located in Hacienda Heights, California. Please browse through our site and find out more about our projects and accomplishments.
