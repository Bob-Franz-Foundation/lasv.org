ABOUT ISAAQ


ISAAQ (Innovative Schools Advancing Air Quality) is an organization of high school teachers in Southern California who are dedicated to educate young people the importance of alternative energy in the future of transportation. Robert Franz is the current chairman of ISAAQ. This organization holds a special challenge, Zero Emissions Vehicles (ZEV), to encourage young people to promote alternative energy in the future.  ISAAQ and the Southern California AQMD co-sponsor the Zero Emission Vehicles (ZEV) Challenge.
