The "Build Your Dream Vehicle" Program was developed by  the Diamler-Chrysler Corporation for high school students in grades 9-12. Its  purpose was to provide students with an opportunity to:
  • Work as part of a team
     
  • Explore an interesting field of work
     
  • Combine the academic knowledge and vocational skills of 
    the students
     
  • Design an environmentally friendly, alternative energy,  futuristic vehicle

The first round of competition is the judging of written reports of the marketing plan, ergonimics, safety features, environmental friendlyness, and drawings of the car. Most groups also send in a 3-D model of their car. In the second round of competition, the finalists from the first  round make presentations to a panel of judges.

Every year students from Los Altos compete in the  compitition. Since Los Altos started participating in the compitition in  1996, we have placed exceptionaly every year. In 1996 we placed 1st, in 1997 and 1998 we placed 1st and 2nd, and in 1999, we swept the competition! 2000 we  had four teams make the finals and we wound up winning 1st and 2nd places. The  first place team, Horizon won the opportunity to go to the national finals copetition in Detroit, Michigain. They ended up winning second place.

In 2001 we had six teams make it to the regional competition. We won first and second place at this years regionals. The first  place team, Serenity, went to the national finals and got second place.
