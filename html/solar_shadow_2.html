From July15 to 25th, 2001, the Los Altos Solar Vehicle Team will be competing in the American Solar Challenge. The American Solar Challenge is  2300 miles of solar raycing that  will challenge teams from around the world. High tech and high efficiency solar cars will cross the Great Plains, climb the Rocky Mountains, and dash over the Great American Desert to the finish line in Southern California.
