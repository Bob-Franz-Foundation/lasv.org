1993 Arizona Public Services Solar & Electric 500
Eng. Tech students constructed "Speed Racer", an ultra-light
electric vehicle, which pompeted in the ultra-light electric
class. Even with the lowest qualifying time, Speed Racer still
finished in the middle of the pack, which included entries from
Solectria Corp. and Arizona State University

1995 Winston Solar Challenge
LASV project "Cool Runnings" is a full scale solar vehicle
designed and constructed by Los Altos Eng. Tech. students
Cool Runnings won the first Winston Solar Challenge in Dallas
, Texas. This 210 mile event was the first national competition
for solar cars constructed by high schools.
