The Los Altos Engineering Academy has a tradition of integrating math and science curriculum with student directed engineering projects. Students apply academic knowledge to solve real world problems. From solar and electric powered cars to human powered airplanes, students learn to use team work to design and construct large scale projects. Associations with Edison, TRW, Hughes and AeroVironments give students access to leading edge technology and expertise. Cooperation with Cal Poly Pomona and Cal State LA has provided academy students with access to some of the best solar car technology in America

Since 1993, the Los Altos engineering program has given students  the
opportunity to demonstrate their abilities in variety of competitions
