The 1997-98 projects the LASV team are working on include:

S-Class
The LASV team is building a junior-size solar bicycle that will
be raced in Topeka, Kansas in May.

Human Powered Airplane (HPA)
A few of the LA Eng. students are beginning construction of a human
powered airplane. They are planning to launch the plane across the
Los Altos football stadium in June.
