1997 Winston Solar Challenge
The team returns to Dallas, Texas to defend their title. This time
they raced with the solar car, "Solar Shadow". They finished the
race in first place.

1997 Chrysler "Build Your Dream Car" Design Competition
LA Eng. Tech. students placed first and second place, giving Los
Altos a $5,000 prize.

Electric Isuzu
A conversion project undertaken by the Alternative Fuels Class, part
of the Engineering Academy, students replaced the internal combustion
drive train with a DC motor and control system. The car was designed to
compete in the Arizona Public Services Electric Vehicle Technology
Competition (EVTC). Students from across America participate in the yearly
competition.
