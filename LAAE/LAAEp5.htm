1996 World Solar Challenge
LASV constructed "Solar Shadow", their second solar vehicle,
to race in the World Solar Challenge in Australia, where they
competed against big corporations and colleges. They were
reorganized for being one of the few who had completed the race

1996 Chrysler "Build Your Dream Car" Design Competition
A team event which required students to design a clean air
concept vehicle, develop a marketing plan and present their
project to Chrysler Corp Exectives. LA Eng. Tech. students
placed three teams in the finals and "Decorum", a hybrid luxury
car took first place, giving Los Altos a $3,000 price.
