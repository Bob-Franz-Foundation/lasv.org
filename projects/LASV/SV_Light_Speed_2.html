Light Speed II

 

Designed and Constructed
1998
First Race:
Solar Bike Rayce USA, S-Class: May 1997, Topeka Kansas

Dimensions
Height: 22 in.
Width: 36 in
Length: 116 in.
Weight: 330 lb.

Solar Array:
Size: 1.2 m

Carbon fiber and fiberglass body with plexiglass windows

Wheels: NewGen Electric Car Wheels
Tires: Bridgestone Electric Car Tires
