3rd MS Robotics Competition


Robots
· The robot's dimensions must not exceed 18” wide X 12” long X 12” high.
· Robots can be built from any or all pieces included within the Lego® MindStorms® kit. Significant limitations include 1 RCX minicomputer and 2 Lego® motors.

Scoring
· To score in a colored area or in a goal, any part of the desired object must be within the vertical projection of the destination.
· Toilet paper tubes must be at an angle greater than 45° to count as being knocked over.

Seeding
· The Seeding Round Competitions will consist of three rounds for each team.
· Each team will automatically be assigned to the “black” side of the board for all seeding rounds.
· Each round will be exactly 60 seconds long in which one team alone will take the field and attempt to score as many points as possible.
· If a robot fails to terminate before the allotted time expires, a score of -1 one will be awarded to that team for that round.
· The average of the top two seeding round scores for each team will be taken and used to determine bracketing for head-to-head.
· Each team will have a few minutes between rounds to make any changes necessary.
· Any team not ready to begin a round will receive a score of -1 for that round.
· In order to receive a valid score, each team must attempt to score points, no sitting around and winning because the other teams have negative points (were such situation to arise that team would also receive a score of -1).
· Each team will receive -1 point for each “white” object in scoring position.

Head-to-Head
· The Head-to-Head rounds of the competition will occur after the Seeding round competition is completed. Placements depend on the performance of the seeding rounds.
· The same rules will apply to the Head-to-Head round that had applied to the Seeding Rounds, such as scoring and time limits.
· The Head-to-Head competitions will be an elimination setup in which those that prevail will be able to move onto the next round inn the winner brs bracket and all losers must move to the losers brackets.
· Once a team, has lost in the losers bracket, that team is eliminated from competition.
