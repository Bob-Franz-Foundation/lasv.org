Load Test

Purpose: To measure the voltage of a power supply as a load is applied. In other words, to measure how long the batteries will last.

We will measure how many volts are running through the board at each 10 minute interval for a closed-circuit board (when the switch is turned on).

Materials:

circuit board
multimeter
timer
pen and paper for recording data

 

Procedures:

1.) Stick the negative (black) probe of the multimeter in between the negative end of the left battery and its battery holder.

2.) Stick the positive (red) probe of the multimeter in between the positive end of the right battery and its battery holder.

3.) Turn the dial of the multimeter to 20 under DCV.

4.) Check the voltage and record.

5.) Get the timer ready.

6.) Turn on the knife switch and immediately record the voltage.

7.) After every 10 minutes, check the multimeter and record the voltage. Repeat this step until the lights get dimmer and dimmer.
