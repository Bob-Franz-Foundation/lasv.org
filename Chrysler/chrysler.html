The "Build Your Dream Vehicle" Program was developed by the Diamler-Chrysler Corporation  for high school students in grades 9-12.  Its purpose was to provide students with an opportunity to:

  • Work as part of a team
  • Explore an interesting field of work
  • Combine the academic knowledge and vocational skills of the students
  • Design an environmentally friendly, alternative energy, futuristic  vehicle

The first round of competition is the judging of written reports of the  marketing plan, ergonimics, safety features, environmental friendlyness, and  drawings of the car.  Most groups also send in a 3-D model of their car.  In the second round of competition, the finalists from the first round make  presentations to a panel of judges.

Every year students from Los Altos  compete in the compitition. Since Los Altos started participating in the  compititionin in 1996, we have placed exceptionaly every year. In 1996 we placed  1st, in 1997 and 1998 we placed 1st and 2nd, and in 1999, we swept the competition!  This year we had four teams make the finals and we wound up winning 1st and 2nd places.

Starting this year, the winning team has the opportunity to go on to a national finals competition in Detroit, Michigain, and our winning team, Horizon, will be going there near the end of May.
