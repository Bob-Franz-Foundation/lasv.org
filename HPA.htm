INTRODUCTION

Los Altos Human Powered Airplane (LAHPA) was an idea started after our success in the Chrysler Dream Car Competition. This project began during Feburary of 1997 and our class was divided into two groups, LASV (Los Altos Solar Vehicle) and LAHPA.

OBJECTIVES

Currently 15 students in LAHPA project. Our current goal is to have the specs for the HPA ready by May 1997, and our ultimate intent is to fly the HPA for 100 meters. We have some materials for HPA, but we still need many more assistance as for design and financial sponsors and supporters. If you are interested or have any suggestions, please contact us at LAHPA@hotmail.com. Thank you.
