Team Leader Video: Ann Chong

Name: Ann Chong

 

Team: Oversee all teams

 

How long you’ve been in Engineering: I’ve been in the 5th period class since sophomore year, but I worked on Build Your Dream Vehicle and Solar Boat during my freshman year. 

 

Leadership Position Description (what you do as the team leader): Project Manager.  As project manager, I work among the teams to establish deadlines and coordinate tasks to reduce wasted time and effort. 

 

What got you interested in Engineering in the first place: I first got interested in Engineering in eighth grade after I listened to a presentation made about the program.  I thought it was very interesting and the fact that it involved alternative energy was the clincher.  I knew that I wanted to get involved so I took the Introduction to Computer Science class the summer before my freshman year. 

 

Favorite thing about Engineering:  The people here and the feeling that you did something significant.  My fellow students are the most down-to-earth people I know.  I can trust them entirely and I can count on them to get their work done.  The feeling that I get when I get a task done or resolve a problem is one of the best feelings I’ve ever experienced.  Not many people have the opportunities that this program provides and I’m eternally grateful for being given the chance to work in the program. 

 

Most memorable moment in Engineering:  My most memorable moment in Engineering is when the solar array that I worked on for the 2006 solar boat, The Leviathan, for about five months was destroyed after the conformal coating cured incorrectly.  I had tabbed individual cells, strung the cells together, attached the strings on the substrate, soldered the interconnects together, put in diodes, and made it work.  When I read the voltage on the day that we finished, I was so happy.  However, it was short-lived, since the conformal coating that should have been clear ended up frosty. 

 

Projects Worked On and what grade you were in:

Build Your Dream Vehicle, Dynamic Innovations - Sparta (9th)

Solar Boat, Corsair (9th)

Build Your Dream Vehicle, Dynamic Innovations - Dodge Neon RO (10th)

Solar Shadow III (10th)

SkillsUSA - VICA Quizbowl (10th)

Build Your Dream Vehicle, Dynamic Innovations - Di400 (11th)

Solar Boat, Leviathan (11th)

Infusion (12th)

DARPA (12th)

Solar Boat (12th)

 

Future Plans:  The engineering academy has developed my preexisting affinity for engineering.  I love making sure processes are efficient and find new solutions to problems.   I hope to attend a four-year university and major in Chemical Engineering.  After getting a Bachelor's degree I want to pursue graduate school.   I eventually want to work with alternative energy and advance the field.
