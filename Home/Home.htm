Welcome to LASV.org, the home page of the Los Altos Academy of Engineering and the Los Altos Solar Vehicle Program. This is a class/club formed by the La Puente Valley ROP and Los Altos High School. This club is run mainly by the students who have dedicated their time to this cause. The school is located in Hacienda Heights, California.
