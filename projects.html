BYDV

BYDV requires students to use mathematics, science, social studies, language arts, technology, and design skills to develop and design a vehicle.

Infusion 

The goal of this project is an Ultra-light, hydrogen fuel cell, single person vehicle that will travel forty miles in one hour using six liters of compressed hydrogen.

 Botball

Autonomous Robots compete in a table-top environment performing various point-scoring activities.

 

Solar Boat

Students design, develop and construct a boat that is powered with solar energy.

 

MS Robotics

Botball competition geared towards middle school students.

 

S-Class

Students work on solar car for race in Kansas, American Solar Challenge, and World Solar Challenge.

 

JPL

Students create projects to complete objectives presented by JPL and compete against other High Schools and JPL employees.
