Solar Shadow Technical Specifications

Dimensions

Length

4.55 m

Width

1.73 m

Height

1.01 m

Weight

340 kg

Solar Array

Dimensions

8 m2 on car, 10 m2 including ext. panel

Output

1100w peak, 700w-900w avg.

Composition

1034 terrestrial grade silicon solar cells
-721 cells on car in two zones of 360 and 361
-313 cells on external charging panel

Efficiency

14% of sunlight converted to electricity

Batteries

8 GNB EV-500 lead-acid 12v batteries in a 96v system.
-3.4Wh discharge rate at 5h
-15.6 kg / battery, total weight of 125 kg.

Motor

Solectria DC brush-type permanent magnet motor - 85% efficient
-rated power 3 hp
-max power 12 hp

Body

Chassis

Dual-layer nomex honeycomb core material w/carbon fiber support and kevlar outer skin

Array Substructure

Foam core material w/fiberglass on the cross ribs and skin and carbon fiber support on longitudinals

Suspension

Front

Modified dual A-arm Works Performance Shocks

Rear

Swing arm, dual wheel, one drive, one brake

Brakes

Primary and secondary hydraulic disc brakes and regen braking

Tires

20 in. (50.8 cm), 100 psi ACS brand BMX tires

Performance

Average speed of 40-50 km/h
Top speed of 65 km/h
