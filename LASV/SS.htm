Solar Shadow, LASVís second major solar vehicle project, was created in 1996 in cooperation with California State Polytechnic University at Pomonaís CaPSET team.  LASV received overwhelming support from our local community in our first Adopt-a-Cell program, and local businesses and manufacturers were also generous with their advice and assets.  This vehicle is especially designed for long distance competitions, which is why it performed well in the 1996 World Solar Challenge in Australia.   After  success in Australia, Solar Shadow came back to the States to compete in the 1997 Winston Solar Challenge in Texas and LASV once again defended its title as National Champions.
