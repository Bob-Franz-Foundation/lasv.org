After winning last years Solar Bike Rayce, USA, S-Class division with our newest car Light Speed, we will be returning this May to defend our title. We also will be racing our electrathon vehicle, Speed Racer, in the national Electrathon Championships this April, possibly introducing our newest project, an electrathon vehicle based on the design of Light Speed.

We are also working on a brand new state of the art Data Aquisistion Unit. The unit will allow our electrical team to monitor and log data on all batteries being tested. Through the process of computer controlled relay boards along with high powered contactors, we will be able to controll the cycling of multiple batteries. A computer will then log the data (taken every 1/2 second) and graph it to be viewed by our electrical team members.
