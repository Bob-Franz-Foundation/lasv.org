Lightspeed was the first vehicle completely engineered and manufactured by students.  It was conceived in the minds of students in 1997 and was raced in the Solar BikeRayce in Kansas, where it became the first vehicle in its class to complete the 100 km race in the time allotted.  After being rebuilt and slightly redesigned in 1998, we took it back to Kansas to defend our title.  Unfortunately, our luck ran out and the car crashed.
