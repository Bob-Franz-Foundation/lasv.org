WHAT IS SOLAR CUP?

Sponsored by The Metropolitan Water District of Southern California, as well as their member agencies, Solar Cup is an education program in which teams from various high schools are educated on water conservation along with renewable energy, through races with solar boats.

BUILDING A SOLAR BOAT

In this competition, students are required to build and race a solar powered boat within the duration of 7 months. Furthermore, the teams are to research and complete technical reports concerning the task, and create a water-related public service announcement.

OUR ROLE IN THE COMPETITION

LAAE particpates in this competition yearly, creating many solar-powered boats over the years, as part of our pursuit to a more cleaner future. Any member willing to put in the effort and show perseverance in this project can join.

OUR TEAMS INVOLVED
