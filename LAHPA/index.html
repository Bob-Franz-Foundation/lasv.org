Welcome to the Los Altos Human Powered Airplane page! LAHPA is a project being worked on by a group of ambitious high school students. The idea for it came about two years ago when several students found some information on human powered airplanes and thought it would be a good challenge to attempt one of their own.
After two years of research, design, planning, and construction, the plane is very close to completion. In fact, we came very close to flying the plane this past summer. Unfortunately, we found several design flaws and ran into some unpredicted problems that kept us from flying the plane on schedule.

According to our sources, no high school in the United States has ever constructed a human powered airplane. This gives us an opportunity to be the first high school to ever accomplish this. Up to this point, we have rceived support from many companies and organizations, mostly in the form of monetary and equipment donations. We are working extremely hard to accomplish our goal. In order for our continued success, we need support from everywhere possible. Thank you for your intrest in our project, and any comments and questons regarding our project would be welcomed.
