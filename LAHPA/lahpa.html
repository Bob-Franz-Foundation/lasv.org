Welcome to the Los  Altos Human Powered Airplane Project website.  Since its inception three years  ago, the Los Altos Human Powered Airplane (HPA) project, also known as  the Grasshopper, has been one of the longest and most challenging  projects undertaken by the students of the Engineering Technology class.

Currently,  we are very close to completing the airplane, and plan to attempt to fly it over  spring break in late April.  However, in order to fly the plane, we need to find  an insurance carrier to insure the plane so that we can reserve a hangar at  March Air Force Base in Riverside.  If you or anyone you know might be able to  help us, please e-mail us with contact information, or call us at (626)  330-1096.
