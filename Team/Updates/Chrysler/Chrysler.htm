Chrysler Team Weekly Update
Feb 11, 2003

With the due date looming just ahead in March, the three teams are working hard to complete the projects. Mr. Kodama was nice enough to check over the notebooks on Feb 4 and will hopefully provide good feedback. With so many other projects this year, the Chrysler teams are putting in extra hours to get their Chrysler project, as well as engineering projects done in time.
